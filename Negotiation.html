Preparation & Planning

The Foundation of Successful Negotiation

Systematic preparation that transforms negotiations from reactive discussions to strategic dialogues. Includes:

  • BATNA analysis (Best Alternative to a Negotiated Agreement)
  • ZOPA identification (Zone of Possible Agreement)
  • Stakeholder mapping and interest analysis
  • Scenario planning and concession strategy
Strategic Application in Complex Deals:

Develop a "Negotiation Preparation Matrix" mapping interests, positions, and alternatives for all parties. Research shows prepared negotiators achieve 35% better outcomes and identify 42% more value-creating opportunities.
