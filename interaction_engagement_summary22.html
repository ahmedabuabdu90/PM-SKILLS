Foundations of Effective Interaction

  • Tailored Communication: Abandon "one-size-fits-all". Customize granularity for executives vs. technical teams.
  • Feedback Loops: Establish mechanisms for continuous, bi-directional feedback (e.g., Retrospectives).
  • Psychological Safety: Make it safe to report errors. Innovation dies where fear of retribution lives.
  • Servant Leadership: Focus on unblocking the team and supporting growth, not just commanding tasks.

Managing Expectations

1

The Challenge

A client was constantly adding new requirements without adjusting the timeline.

2

The Application
